Poren Chiang is a digital law researcher and a software developer based in Taiwan.

His research agenda focuses on electronic voting and digital transformation of administrative procedures. He also writes on a wide range of topics including privacy and electronic device searches, artificial intelligence, open source license, social media regulations, open data policies, and freedom of information.

Poren has been an active contributor of FLOSS community. He also co-founded SITCON and stood as its chief Organizer, a tech education initiative to support young programming self-starters.
